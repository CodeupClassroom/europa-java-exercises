Break and Continue

break and continue statements can be used to alter the flow of a loop, independent of the loop's condition.
A break statement is used to exit the current loop.
In the case of multiple nested loops, a labeled break statement may be used to differentiate which loop to exit.
A continue statement is used to skip any remaining statements in the current loop and jump to the top of the current loop.
A labeled continue statement may be used to jump to the top of a labeled loop.
