For Loop

A for loop can be thought of as a common case of a while loop
A single statement needs to happen before the loop starts.
And a single statement needs to happen after the body of the loop during each iteration.
Basic Structure
A for loop is useful when we know ahead of time how many times our loop needs to execute. It is also commonly used with arrays.
