Review

Conditionals and control statements are how a computer makes decision.

A boolean expression evaluates to either true or false.

Logical operators allow us to combine multiple boolean values to produce a single boolean value.

The logical operators are: '&&', '||', '&', '|', and '!'

don't use '==' to compare objects.

When comparing objects, use the .equals() method.

Control structures allow us to control the flow of execution of our code.

Control structures fall into two categories: Selection Statements (Conditionals) Loops

The control statements are if and switch.

The loop structures are while, do-while, and for.

break and continue statements can be used to alter the flow of a loop, independent of the loop's condition.
